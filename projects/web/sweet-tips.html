Sweet Tips

Explore practical tips and baking hacks to make your desserts turn out perfect every time!

Keep Cookies Soft

Store cookies with a slice of bread in an airtight container. The bread will keep them moist and fresh longer.

Fluffier Cakes

Sift flour before mixing to create lighter, fluffier cakes with a smooth texture.

Perfect Caramel

Always swirl the pan instead of stirring when making caramel to avoid crystallization.

No More Sticky Dough

Dust your hands with powdered sugar instead of flour when handling sticky doughs like for alfajores.

Richer Chocolate Flavor

Add a pinch of espresso powder to your chocolate desserts for a deeper, bolder taste.
